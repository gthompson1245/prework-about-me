What are your favorite hobbies?

I am most definitely a hobbyist. I have what sometimes seems like 100 hobbies. I am an avid amateur radio operator/listener - my callsign is KN4YIJ - My knowledge is evolving every day as I explore the countless aspects of the hobby - it is rich, rewarding and challenging. I am often surrounded by much more experienced operators and their (your!) knowledge is what drives me to keep exploring. 73! My favorite thing to do is ride my Yamaha YZ450F at full throttle (off road) - I love speed, I love adrenaline and I enjoy sitting back after a good day and building electronics or getting out in the trees to improve my antenna any way I can - it has been an interesting process. Currently, I have an inverted v suspended in space between the house and an Eastern Red Cedar tree. The apex is at 28' with 53' bare wire legs at approx. 130deg - 25ft of window-line straight to an air core balun of coax to the antenna tuner inside a Xiegu G90 transceiver. According to my measurements - the antenna is nearly.. good. I have hit Toronto on 8 watts. So you be the judge. As I move forward in the hobby I intend on exploring other antenna systems, transceivers, linear amps etc. I enjoy working with my hands and my mind. I build Arduino widgets whenever I discover a need - no matter how silly. I enjoy IoT and network integration. When in the garage, I'm either welding something, honing my technique or wrenching on a motorcycle.
